About Us

Trusted Yamaha Dealer with Over 10 Years of Excellence

Rudra Automobiles is the authorised dealer for Yamaha Motor India, offering an impressive lineup of high-performance motorcycles and scooters that cater to the needs of discerning riders. As the go-to destination for Yamaha enthusiasts, Rudra Automobiles specializes in super sports bikes like the R15 and MT15, which deliver an exhilarating riding experience thanks to their powerful engines, advanced engineering, and sleek, aerodynamic designs. These bikes are a true testament to Yamaha's commitment to innovation and performance, providing riders with the perfect balance of speed, handling, and style.

In addition to the super sports bikes, Rudra Automobiles also proudly showcases Yamaha's impressive range of super performer scooters, including the popular Fascino and RayZR models. These scooters are designed to combine the practicality and convenience of a two-wheeler with the performance and features that discerning customers demand. From the Fascino's retro-inspired aesthetics and smooth, efficient powertrain to the RayZR's sporty good looks and nimble handling, Rudra Automobiles offers a diverse selection of Yamaha scooters to suit the varied needs and preferences of its customers.

With over a decade of experience in the industry, Rudra Automobiles has established itself as a trusted and reliable bike dealer, earning the complete trust and satisfaction of its customers. The company's commitment to exceptional customer service, combined with its expertise in Yamaha's world-class products, has made Rudra Automobiles the go-to destination for anyone seeking the ultimate in two-wheeled performance and style. Whether you're in the market for a thrilling super sports bike or a practical, yet stylish, super performer scooter, Rudra Automobiles is the name you can count on to deliver an unparalleled riding experience.
